Bouncing Ball Game

Rules

  • 1. When the ball encounters a strawberry, it earns 5 points.
  • 2. If the ball hits the wall, it loses 1 point.
  • 3. When the ball hits the right or left wall, its diameter increases.
  • 4. When the ball hits the top or bottom wall, its diameter decreases.
  • 5. The game ends when the ball completely fills the canvas or disappears.
  • 6. Try to score as many points as possible within 60 seconds!
